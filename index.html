Tweet Hide World Cup 2018

Chrome Extension
July 2018

Now, it is the period of World Cup 2018 and news about it are everywhere. And, are you annoyed of seeing them, or do you hate football? If yes, then this Chrome Extension is for you. It hides World Cup 2018 related tweets. Just install it, and do not let tweets get on your nerves!
